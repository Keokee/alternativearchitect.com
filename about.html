Joseph Henry Wythe

  • A Soul attuned to the wonders of NATURE to achieve the heights of creative imagination
  • A journeyman carpenter while studying architecture
  • Studied architecture under Bruce Goff, close friend of Frank Lloyd Wright
  • Taught architecture at Oklahoma University and Monterey Peninsula College
  • Certified by the National Council of Architectural Registration Boards (NCARB)
  • Designer of many outstanding projects - commercial, institutional and residential - since 1947
  • What is the real reason to be an architect? Is it not to serve the highest aspirations of mankind?
  • "Joe Wythe...one of the greatest living architects." *
  • "For five productive decades, Joe Wythe has always chosen to go a step beyond and reach for beauty. He has consistently chosen to make a positive difference in the lives of others." *
* (Quotations by Arthur Dyson, Architect)
